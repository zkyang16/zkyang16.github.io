I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at Clemson University, focusing on design of polymer nanocomposites and bioinspired functionally graded materials using multiscale modeling and machine learning. I received my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Clemson University in May 2025. Prior to that, I earned both my Master’s and Bachelor’s degrees in engineering back in China. In addition, I have industry experience from my time at <a href="https://www.dji.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">DJI</a>, a globally recognized leader in consumer-grade and agricultural drone manufacturing.
