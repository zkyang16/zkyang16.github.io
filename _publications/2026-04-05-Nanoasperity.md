title: "Nanoasperity-driven reinforcement and viscoelastic modulation in polymer nanocomposites: A coarse-grained molecular dynamics study"
collection: publications
category: manuscripts
excerpt: 'This study uses coarse-grained molecular dynamics to investigate how nanofiller surface roughness and interfacial strength dictate the mechanical performance of PMMA nanocomposites. The results show that deep nanoasperities enhance stiffness through a synergy of mechanical interlocking and polymer confinement, provided that interfacial interactions are strong. Conversely, weak interactions lead to interfacial slip and diminished reinforcement. Viscoelastic analysis further reveals that damping behavior is determined by the competition between interfacial confinement and heterogeneous chain dynamics. These findings establish that surface geometry and interfacial mechanics must be coupled to tailor the stiffness and energy dissipation of polymer nanocomposites.'
