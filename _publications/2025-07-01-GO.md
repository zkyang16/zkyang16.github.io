title: "Impact of graphene oxide arrangement on the mechanical and viscoelastic properties of polymer nanocomposites"
collection: publications
category: manuscripts
excerpt: 'This study uses coarse-grained molecular dynamics to examine how graphene oxide (GO) arrangement, interfacial interaction, and oxidation profiles dictate the mechanical and viscoelastic properties of PMMA nanocomposites. While separated GO sheets optimize stiffness through enhanced nanoconfinement, stacked configurations activate an inter-sheet sliding mechanism that significantly improves damping and energy dissipation. These findings reveal that GO stacking can be strategically leveraged to tailor the trade-off between structural reinforcement and viscoelastic performance in polymer design.'
