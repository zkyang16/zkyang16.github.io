title: "Porous nanoparticles overcome the conventional stiffness-damping tradeoff in polymer nanocomposites"
collection: publications
category: manuscripts
excerpt: 'This study utilizes coarse-grained molecular dynamics to demonstrate how nanoparticle surface geometry, specifically smooth, corrugated, and porous configurations, dictates the mechanical and viscoelastic properties of PMMA nanocomposites. Porous nanoparticles provide the greatest enhancement to stiffness and shear moduli by inducing unique structural confinement and molecular dynamic heterogeneity. Notably, these configurations allow for simultaneous improvements in both stiffness and damping. This effectively overcomes the traditional mechanical tradeoff and offers a pathway for designing high-performance, multifunctional nanocomposites.'
