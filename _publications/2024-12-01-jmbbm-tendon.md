title: "Understanding the effects of mineralization and structure on the mechanical properties of tendon-bone insertion using mesoscale computational modeling"
collection: publications
category: manuscripts
excerpt: 'This study utilizes mesoscale computational modeling to investigate how mineralization levels and branching fiber architectures dictate the mechanical performance of tendon-bone entheses. By simulating tensile loading across various configurations, the research identifies trade-offs between strength, stiffness, and toughness, identifying an optimal mineralization scale for energy absorption. These insights into structure-function relationships offer a framework for the biomimetic design of high-performance interfaces joining soft and hard materials.'
