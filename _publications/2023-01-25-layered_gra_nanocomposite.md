title: "Investigation of dynamic impact responses of layered polymer-graphene nanocomposite films using coarse-grained molecular dynamics simulations"
collection: publications
category: manuscripts
excerpt: 'Polymer nanocomposite films show superior energy dissipation under impact, but the underlying mechanisms have remained poorly understood. This study uses coarse-grained molecular dynamics simulations to investigate stress wave propagation and dynamic failure in layered PMMA-graphene nanocomposite films during piston impact. Results reveal that PMMA crazing — triggered when a critical piston velocity is reached — significantly enhances energy dissipation, and that sufficient PMMA-graphene interfacial strength is required to activate crazing rather than interfacial separation. These findings offer key insights into how nanointerfaces and nanostructures govern the energy dissipation capability of layered nanocomposite films.'
